# ENTERPRISE LAYER

Strategic vision and operational framework for OneHeart as a **Real-Life Operating System for National Impact**.

---

## Vision

**Convert daily real-world actions into measurable social-economic value via AI + Game Mechanics**

OneHeart transforms citizens into active contributors to national development. Every action—from local volunteering to global research—is quantified, verified, and rewarded with economic and social returns.

---

## Mission

Enable **citizen participation at scale** by:
1. Making real-world impact **visible and rewarding** (via XP, tokens, badges).
2. **Verifying authenticity** (via Integrity AI) to maintain system credibility.
3. **Aggregating local actions** into national and global outcomes.
4. **Funding development** through impact-driven investment and sponsorship.

---

## Business Domains

### 1. Social Impact Domain
**Goal:** Mobilize citizens for community betterment.
- Cleanup campaigns, disaster relief, elder support
- Peer mentoring, skill-sharing
- Civic participation

### 2. National Development Domain
**Goal:** Drive sustainable nation-building initiatives.
- Infrastructure projects, research collaboration
- Skill development, workforce training
- Economic opportunity matching

### 3. Tourism & Culture Domain
**Goal:** Boost tourism revenue while preserving culture.
- Cultural guide missions, heritage conservation
- Tourism point experiences, local storytelling
- Cultural exchange programs

### 4. Citizen XP & Rewards Domain
**Goal:** Gamify progress and incentivize engagement.
- Real-world actions → XP/tokens (via Proof Layer + Impact Engine)
- Tokens redeemable in marketplace
- Progression unlocks higher-impact opportunities

## Stakeholder Ecosystem

Citizens (Players) → Government Units → Tourism Entities → Sponsors/Partners → Global R&D Collaborators

| Stakeholder | Role | Incentive |
|---|---|---|
| Citizens | Perform actions, build skills | XP, tokens, badges, employment |
| Government | Policy, data, missions | National KPI achievement, citizen engagement |
| Tourism Entities | Tourism missions, rewards | Visitor volume, revenue, brand promotion |
| Sponsors | Funding, branded rewards | Brand association, ROI tracking, ESG impact |
| R&D Partners | Joint missions, technology | Innovation, publications, scaling |

---

## Operating Model

### Revenue Streams
1. **Sponsor Contributions** (50% primary) → 50% rewards, 30% operations, 20% fund seeding
2. **Impact Monetization** (data licensing, impact verification as service)
3. **Marketplace Commission** (% fee on token redemptions)

### Unit Economics (Pilot: 1,000 players)
- Avg player sponsorship/month: $10
- Total monthly budget: $10,000
- Avg reward per player: $5
- Operational cost per player: $3
- Gross margin: 20%
